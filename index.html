Detailed Information: Streaming Data Insights

Objective: Uncover trends in streaming content across major platforms to analyze viewer preferences and industry trends from 1912 to 2023.

Volume: Analyzed 22,619 titles and 326,177 credits across platforms.

Tools & Technologies:

  • Python (Jupyter Notebook) for data scraping and preprocessing.
  • SQL (Snowflake) for complex data queries and analysis.
  • Tableau for interactive visualizations.

Insights:

  • Revealed Amazon's extensive content library versus Disney's targeted niche approach.
  • Highlighted Netflix's balanced approach in content diversity and high ratings.

Decision-Making Impact: Assisted streaming platforms in tailoring their content offerings for maximum engagement.

Team Collaboration: Worked within a team of 5, dividing tasks for a comprehensive approach.
